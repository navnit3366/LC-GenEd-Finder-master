Find the GenEd That is Right for You!

This is a web application that searches through the courses offered at Luther College and allows you to search through those courses based on the All College General Education requirements that they satisfy. You may either click a GenEd on the left to search by a single requirement, or select mulitple GenEds on the right to search by multiple requirements. Alternatively, you can search by keywords, course title, course number or department.
